跨电AI自动化 Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how 跨电AI自动化 handles information when a TikTok account owner authorizes the app through TikTok Login Kit and related TikTok Developer APIs.

1. Information we collect

We collect only the information needed to provide the authorized TikTok integration and e-commerce workflow:

  • TikTok account identity and profile information provided through user.info.basic and user.info.profile, such as open id, display name, avatar, profile links, bio description, and verification status where provided by TikTok.
  • TikTok account statistics provided through user.info.stats, such as follower count, following count, likes count, and video count.
  • Video and posting data needed for Content Posting API workflows, including approved video assets, upload status, publish id, public video identifiers, and posting status.
  • Public video list and metrics references provided through video.list, used to match the authorized seller's public videos to product campaigns and internal reporting.
  • Operational records such as campaign names, product references, timestamps, authorization status, and audit notes generated by the service workflow.

2. Information we do not collect

We do not collect TikTok passwords, two-factor authentication codes, payment card details, identity documents, unrelated private messages, or unrelated personal content. The service does not ask users to enter TikTok passwords into our application.

3. How we use information

We use the information to authenticate the authorized TikTok account, display the connected profile in the seller dashboard, prepare and upload seller-approved videos, check upload or publish status, associate public TikTok videos with product campaigns, and maintain operational reporting for the seller's e-commerce workflow.

4. Tokens and security

OAuth access tokens, refresh tokens, client secrets, and other credentials are treated as confidential runtime secrets. They are not displayed in the user interface, demo video, public pages, logs, or reports. Access is limited to the systems and operators needed to provide the integration.

5. Sharing and disclosure

We send information to TikTok only as required to use TikTok Developer APIs selected and authorized by the account owner. We do not sell personal information. We may use infrastructure or storage providers to operate the service, but only for hosting, security, logging, and workflow functions needed to provide the application.

6. Retention

We retain operational records only as long as needed for account authorization, video publishing workflows, campaign tracking, audit, troubleshooting, or legal and platform compliance. OAuth tokens are removed or rotated when no longer needed, when access is revoked, or when a security or operational event requires rotation.

7. User choices

A TikTok account owner can revoke app authorization through TikTok settings or by contacting the operator of 跨电AI自动化. After revocation, the service will stop using TikTok API access for that account, subject to any limited records needed for audit, compliance, or security.

8. Children

The service is intended for business operators and authorized TikTok Shop sellers. It is not directed to children.

9. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy when the service, TikTok API scopes, legal requirements, or operational practices change. The effective date above indicates the current version.
