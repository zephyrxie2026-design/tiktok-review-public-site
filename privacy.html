Privacy Policy

CrossBorder AI Video uses TikTok Developer APIs only after the TikTok account owner authorizes the app through OAuth.

Data used

  • Basic/profile/stat account information granted by user.info.basic, user.info.profile, and user.info.stats.
  • Video publishing status and identifiers granted by Content Posting API scopes.
  • Owned-video metrics granted by video.list.

Data handling

Access tokens and secrets are not displayed in the user interface or demo video. Internal reporting stores only operational status, public video identifiers, and metrics needed for e-commerce reporting.

Data not collected

The service does not collect TikTok passwords, 2FA codes, payment details, or unrelated personal content.
