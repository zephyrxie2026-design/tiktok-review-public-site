Terms of Service

CrossBorder AI Video is an internal cross-border e-commerce operations dashboard for authorized operators. The TikTok integration is used only with TikTok accounts authorized through TikTok OAuth in the Developer Sandbox or approved production environment.

Permitted use

  • Connect an authorized TikTok account through Login Kit.
  • Upload or publish approved product videos through TikTok Content Posting API.
  • Read owned-video metrics through TikTok Display API where the account has granted the relevant scopes.

Restrictions

The service does not collect TikTok passwords, bypass TikTok OAuth, bypass review, automate 2FA, scrape private data, or publish without account authorization.
